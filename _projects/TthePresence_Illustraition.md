When creating an immersive board game experience, the game mechanics, story, and artwork need to go hand in hand. Since 2018, I have been consistently working on atmospheric card illustrations and other game components for Presence to help players immerse themselves in the world of this Victorian horror board game.

Below, you can see some of the artwork I have created for the game. In total, I have made over 120 unique illustrations for it.
